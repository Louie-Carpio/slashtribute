Saul "Slash" Hudson

Lead Guitarist for Guns 'N Roses

Here's a timeline of Slash's life
  • 1965 - Born in Hampstead, London.
  • 1981 - Joined first band, Tidus Sloan.
  • 1985 - Joins Guns 'N Roses with Axel Rose and Izzy Stradlin
  • 1987 - Released debut album, Appetite for Destruction
  • 1988 - Sweet Child O' Mine hits US No. 1
  • 1994 - Formed GNR side project, Slash's Pit
  • 1996 - Leaving Guns 'N Roses
  • 2002 - Reunion with Duff Mckagan and formation of Velvet Revolver
  • 2009 - present - Solo album and collaboration with Myles Kennedy and the Conspirators
  • 2016 - present - Return to Guns 'N Roses

And, so, when I picked up the guitar, suddenly, just playing a couple of notes really, really spoke to me. It was almost like I should have been doing it prior to that. You know, it was something that just felt really natural. --Slash
